What makes a triangle?

A triangle is made of three ligns each of which is given a length by a given number. When the addition of two sides length is less or equal to the third line's length a trianlge cannot be made

An equilateral Triangle

You will git an equilateral triangle if all the sides/lines length are equal.

An Isocele triangle

A scalene triangle is a triangle which fulfills the conditions for a trianglem but of which no side is eaual to the other.
